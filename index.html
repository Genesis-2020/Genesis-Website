ABOUT US

Genesis_SIG is formed in to provide an ecosystem to bring tech enthusiasts, learners, technology geeks and researchers together to learn, enhance, collaborate, exhibit and extend their knowledge in their prospective fields.

  • We strongly believe that prospective students and active learners should always be learning and be aware of the emerging technologies, applications and developments in various feilds of science and technology.
  • We want to provide a platform for such people so that one can position themseleves to take the advantage of emerging trends and equip themseleves with the proper skillset to face and compete with the outside world.
  • Our prior focus and aim are to discuss, learn, develop, work on new projects and contribute to R&D as a community and bring an industrial approach in-campus.
